About.

Digital Product & Service Designer currently based in Lisbon. Before descovering my passion about UX, I used to work as corporate lawyer. Crazy, heh?

For me, design is, above everything, finding problems and giving them solutions. And that is what I do when I design: understanding the user’s problems and meeting them with the businesses’ necessities through awesome digital products and services.

I cover the whole Design Thinking process: research for empathizising and defining the problems of the user, ideation of solutions, prototyping and testing with users.

Apart from designing, I work as Teacher Assistant for Ironhack Lisbon and I lead UX, UI and Design Thinking workshops.
